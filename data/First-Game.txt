<b>Hardware</b> Character sheets, move sheets, dice, pencils, blank paper (for GM <i>and for players</i>).
<b>Moves</b> 2D6 + Modifier.<ul><li>10+ success.</li><li>7-9 success with complications.</li><li>6- you won't like it.</li></ul>All dice rolls are done by the players. Pay attention to basic moves, each stat has a purpose.
<b>Scores and Modifiers</b><ul><li>Score: Determines modifier. Also used for resources like hit points and discounts on services.</li><li>Modifier: Added to move rolls and certain resources. Eg: STR is the modifier derived from Strength.</li></ul>

<b>Introduce Your Character</b> Ask questions about the character's traits and history.
<b>Bonds</b> Stress the value of Aid or Interfere move (roll+number of bonds). Discover why the characters have a history that is unresolved.
<b>Take a Break</b> Brainstorm. <i>Steed's own recipe: Create name tags for characters and hirelings to hang on GM screen (and hang +/- 1 forwards on characters). I use blank business cards folded in half and write on both sides.</i>
